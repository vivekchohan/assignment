ABOUT SAI ROCK BEACH HOTEL

A LEADING BEACH HOTEL OF KENYA'S NORTH COASTAL STRIP

Justly famed for its exceptional standards of efficiency service and hospitality, elegantly sophisicated , Sai Rock Hotel is a destination for conferencing and leisure in the Kenya's North coastal strip.

Despite its pivotal location in the outskirts of Mombasa Island to the Nothern region , Sairock Hotel remains true to its title and offers world class amidst the Africa's most vibrant coastal cities.

Sairock Beach Hotel & Spa is renowned for fine Indian and Chinese dishes alongside Local Cuisine and selections which remain unique served at our various restaurants which are universally acknowledged as one of Kenya's finest.

The Hotel also offers a Health Club and Spa in addition to the sumptuos mouth watering dishes and drinks at the Harry potters stylish lobby bar and the beach terrace.

Mombasa's undisputed buisness venue of choice for visiting executives and regional co-operate planners alike, the Sairock Beach Hotel offers an unrivalled choice of executive suites , global connectivity , buisness support , conference planning , product launch expertise and high profile cooperate event venues.

GETTING THERE

Our luxury Hotel is located overlooking the famous Mombasa Bamburi Nature Trail along the Mombasa Malindi Road in Kenya's North Coast opposite Bamburi Police Station.

It is just a 15 minute walk from Bamburi Portland Cement factory to the north and a few minutes walk from Bamburi Police Station doubling extra security at our facility .

By road Sai Rock Hotel is 30 minutes drive from Moi International Airport and only 15 minute drive from Mombasa City to the North Coast along the Mombasa Malindi Rd.
